New York City Sign Violation

New York City Department of Buildings and New York City Environmental Control Board issues violations for all types of signs, awnings and cannopies that are in violation of the New York City Administrative Codes.

These violations apply to signs:
  • Installed without a sign permit.
  • Not using a New York City licensed sign hanger.
  • Exceeding the zoning regulations.
  • Lighted signs, landmarks and multiple infractions of the codes.
The DOB and the ECB can issue multiple violations to the same sign until the violations are corected and cleared.

These sign violations can only be coreccted by a licensed New York City sign hanger.

Tru-Art Sign Company is a licensed New York City master sign hanger, and licensed sign hanger, we are able to correct any of these issues.
